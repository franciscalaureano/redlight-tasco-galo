History

Welcome to Tasco do Galo, a lively tavern renowned for its home-style cooking, generous portions, and affordable prices. Our charming and charismatic staff is dedicated to creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for all guests. Step into our cozy space, where friends and families gather to enjoy delicious meals, sip on red wine or refreshing beer, and engage in animated conversations. With its rustic charm and welcoming ambiance, Tasca do Galo is the perfect place to create lasting memories and savor the simple pleasures of good food and great company.

At Tasco do Galo, we celebrate the traditions of hearty cuisine and vibrant camaraderie. Our tables, covered with paper towels, bear the whimsical and witty musings of our loyal patrons, adding a touch of creativity to the dining experience. Join us as we embrace the joy of shared meals, lively banter, and the occasional sing-along. Whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor, Tasco do Galo promises an unforgettable journey of flavors, laughter, and heartfelt moments in a warm and familial setting.
